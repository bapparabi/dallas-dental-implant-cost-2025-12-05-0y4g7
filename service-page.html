The Dental Implant Process

Understanding each step helps you prepare mentally and financially. Here's what to expect from consultation to final restoration.

1

Consultation & Planning

Comprehensive exam, 3D imaging, and personalized treatment plan with transparent cost breakdown.

2

Implant Placement

Surgical placement of titanium implant posts. Performed under local anesthesia or sedation for comfort.

3

Healing Period

3-6 months for osseointegration—the implant fuses with your jawbone for permanent stability.

4

Final Restoration

Custom crown, bridge, or denture attached. Your new teeth look, feel, and function like natural teeth.

Why Choose Dental Implants?

Permanent Solution

Unlike dentures, implants are designed to last a lifetime with proper care. No adhesives, no slipping.

Preserve Bone Health

Implants stimulate your jawbone like natural tooth roots, preventing bone loss and facial changes.

Natural Look & Feel

Eat, speak, and smile with confidence. Implants function exactly like your natural teeth.

Protect Adjacent Teeth

No grinding down healthy teeth for bridges. Implants stand independently.

Success Rate

98% success rate

Dental implants have one of the highest success rates of any surgical procedure. With proper care, they can last 25+ years.
